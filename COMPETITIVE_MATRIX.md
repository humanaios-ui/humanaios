## Battle Cards (Quick Reference)

### "Why not just use Braintrust?"
**Answer:** "Braintrust is excellent for AI monitoring. But when your agent detects it needs someone to verify a delivery or attend a meeting, Braintrust can only log it. HumanAIOS actually routes that task to a human worker and tracks it to completion. We integrate both."

### "Why not just use RentAHuman directly?"
**Answer:** "RentAHuman is great for manual task delegation. But with HumanAIOS, your AI agents automatically detect when they need human help, route the task to RentAHuman (or our marketplace), track costs, and log everything in one system. No manual intervention needed."

### "OpenAI just launched Frontier - why do we need you?"
**Answer:** "Frontier helps you build and govern agents. HumanAIOS helps when those agents need humans. Think of it as complementary - they handle agent identity and permissions, we handle AI-human workflow orchestration. Many customers will use both."

### "Can't we just build this ourselves?"
**Answer:** "You could, but you'd need to build: (1) agent monitoring infrastructure, (2) human marketplace integration, (3) routing logic, (4) cost optimization, (5) enterprise security. That's 6+ months and 3+ engineers. Or you can integrate HumanAIOS in 5 minutes and focus on your core product."

### "This sounds expensive - what's the ROI?"
**Answer:** "Our customers see ROI in weeks. Example: A customer service AI agent costs $0.01/interaction. When it needs human help (10% of cases), that interaction costs $5 if handled manually. With HumanAIOS auto-routing to optimized human workers, it costs $2. At 10,000 interactions/month, that's $3,000/month saved. Our Pro plan is $799."
