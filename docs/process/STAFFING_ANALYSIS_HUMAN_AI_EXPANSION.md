## üîç MARKET BACKGROUND CHECK PROTOCOL

### **You Asked:** "When considering applicants, let's include market background check"

**EXCELLENT IDEA** ‚úÖ

**Standard Vetting Protocol for ALL Future Hires/Contractors:**

### **STEP 1: LinkedIn Research (5 min)**
- Profile exists and is active?
- Employment history consistent?
- Recommendations/endorsements present?
- Skills match what they claim?
- Professional profile photo?
- **RED FLAGS:** No profile, inconsistent history, no endorsements, unprofessional

### **STEP 2: Online Presence Check (10 min)**
- Google search: "[Name] + [Company]"
- Personal website (if they have one)
- GitHub (if technical role)
- Twitter/X (if they mention social media)
- Portfolio (if relevant to role)
- **RED FLAGS:** Nothing found, negative reviews, scams/spam, unprofessional content

### **STEP 3: Company Verification (5 min)**
- Does their company exist?
- Website functional and professional?
- LinkedIn company page exists?
- Recent activity/clients?
- **RED FLAGS:** No website, inactive company, suspicious business model

### **STEP 4: Reference/Review Check (5 min)**
- RentAHuman reviews (if applicable)
- LinkedIn recommendations
- Clutch/Upwork reviews (if freelancer)
- Ask for references (if significant role)
- **RED FLAGS:** No reviews, all 1-star, refusal to provide references

### **STEP 5: Scam Detection (2 min)**
- Crypto/investment pitches = SCAM ‚ùå
- "Too good to be true" = SCAM ‚ùå
- Upfront payment required = SCAM ‚ùå
- Pressure tactics = SCAM ‚ùå
- Poor grammar/spelling = Potential SCAM ‚ö†Ô∏è

**Total Time:** 25-30 minutes per candidate
**Worth It:** Absolutely (protects time, money, mission)
