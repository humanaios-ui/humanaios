### **STEP 5: SCAM DETECTION (2 min)**

**Critical Red Flags:**

**Immediate Disqualification (ANY of these = SCAM):**
- [ ] Crypto/investment pitch in communication
- [ ] Requests money upfront before work
- [ ] "Too good to be true" promises
- [ ] Pressure tactics ("limited time", "act now")
- [ ] Poor grammar/spelling throughout communication
- [ ] Email from free domain (@gmail, @yahoo) when claiming big company
- [ ] Refuses video call or voice verification
- [ ] Price dramatically below market rate (red flag)
- [ ] Asks for personal information (SSN, bank account) prematurely

**Warning Signs (Multiple = Higher Risk):**
- [ ] Very new account on platform (<3 months)
- [ ] No verifiable work history
- [ ] Generic/templated communication
- [ ] Dodges specific questions
- [ ] Claims expertise in everything
