## üö® EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS

### Scenario 1: Revenue Drop (>30% decline for 2 months)

**Immediate Actions:**
1. Pause all Lasting Light expansion
2. Reduce HumanAIOS burn to essentials
3. Prioritize family runway extension
4. Analyze cause (market, product, execution?)
5. Develop recovery plan before resuming mission spending

**Decision Rule:** Family comes first. Always.

---

### Scenario 2: Unexpected Expense (>$10K urgent need)

**Response:**
1. Assess source (family, business, mission?)
2. Draw from appropriate reserves first
3. Pause expansion in affected area
4. Rebuild reserves before continuing
5. Review to prevent recurrence

**Decision Rule:** Emergency fund exists for emergencies. Use it, then rebuild.

---

### Scenario 3: Major Opportunity (partnership, grant, investment)

**Evaluation:**
1. Does it align with vision?
2. Does it compromise family security?
3. Does it create unsustainable obligations?
4. Does it serve both revenue AND mission?
5. Does discernment feel clear?

**Decision Rule:** If any answer is concerning, decline or delay until clarity emerges.

## üí° PHILOSOPHICAL COMMITMENTS

### Abundance Mindset

**We believe:**
- There is enough for everyone (family AND mission)
- Scarcity thinking leads to bad decisions
- Generosity flows from stability
- Patience yields better outcomes than desperation

**Therefore:**
- We don't launch from scarcity
- We don't sacrifice one good for another
- We trust the timeline that serves wholeness
- We celebrate small wins as progress

---

### Stewardship Not Ownership

**We recognize:**
- This vision is bigger than us
- Money is a tool, not the goal
- We are stewards of resources
- Future generations will benefit or suffer from our choices

**Therefore:**
- We manage funds with care and transparency
- We reinvest for long-term impact
- We build systems that outlast us
- We consider 7-generation consequences

---

### Integration Not Separation

**We reject:**
- "Business over here, mission over there" thinking
- Profit at expense of purpose
- Growth at expense of health
- Success at expense of family

**We embrace:**
- Business AS mission (tech funds recovery)
- Profit AND purpose (not either/or)
- Growth AND health (sustainable pace)
- Success AND family (foundation enables impact)
