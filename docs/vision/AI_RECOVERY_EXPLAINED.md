## üí≠ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: Isn't AI going to take all the jobs?**  
A: AI will transform work, yes. But it also creates entirely new categories of work‚Äîtasks that AI needs humans to complete. We're building the infrastructure to connect humans to that work.

**Q: Why focus on recovery? Why not just job training?**  
A: Because unemployment and addiction are deeply connected. Job loss leads to substance use. Substance use prevents employment. You can't solve one without addressing the other. Our model integrates both.

**Q: Is this just gig work? How is it different from Uber?**  
A: Key differences: (1) Much higher pay ($50-175/hour), (2) Integrated support (healthcare, recovery, community), (3) Cooperative ownership (workers become member-owners), (4) Meaningful work (enabling AI to serve humanity).

**Q: Can this really scale?**  
A: Yes. The more AI is deployed, the more human tasks are generated. Our business model (B2B SaaS) is infinitely scalable. The recovery model is proven and replicable. The cooperative structure is time-tested globally.

**Q: What if I don't have addiction issues? Can I still participate?**  
A: Yes! Lasting Light serves anyone needing integrated healthcare, meaningful work, and community connection. Recovery-centered means we prioritize those in need, but the model serves everyone.

**Q: Isn't this just treating symptoms? What about root causes?**  
A: We're addressing root causes: economic insecurity, lack of healthcare access, social isolation, loss of meaning. By providing integrated support and cooperative economics, we're changing the conditions that create suffering.

**Q: How is this funded?**  
A: HumanAIOS (the tech platform) generates revenue by helping enterprises deploy AI more effectively. That revenue funds Lasting Light's recovery and healthcare services. It's a regenerative loop.

**Q: Can I start this in my community?**  
A: Yes! We're developing a replication playbook (available 2027). You'll need local leadership, funding, and community buy-in. We'll provide the model, training, and peer learning network.
